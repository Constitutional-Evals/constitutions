# Governance (interim)

**Status: interim.** A formal governance process is intentionally deferred. The proposal
scopes expert co-authoring (e.g. domain scholars per tradition) and a regular update cadence
for the anchor set as follow-up work, out of scope for the current phase.

## Interim arrangement (current phase, ~through the Project-1 paper)

- The constitution set is maintained by the **core team** (see `.github/CODEOWNERS`).
- Changes enter via pull request and are reviewed by the `governance` team.
- The **frozen-spec contract** (`VERSIONING.md`) is in force now: no silent edits; every
  change bumps a version and adds a changelog entry.
- The **anchor basis is treated as locked** once fit. Anchor edits are high-friction by
  design: they invalidate the basis and force a refit + new `basis_version`.

## Planned (post-paper, target ~2027)

- Per-tradition expert co-authors for the anchor constitutions.
- A documented amendment process (proposal -> review -> release) on a regular cadence.
- A standing review body distinct from the engineering team.

Until that process is established, describe this set accurately as a research benchmark under
active development — **not** yet a neutral, expert-governed standard.
